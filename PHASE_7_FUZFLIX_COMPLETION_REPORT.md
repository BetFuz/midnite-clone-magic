## Betting Mechanics

### Prediction Types
1. **Binary Markets:** Yes/No outcomes (2 options)
2. **Multiple Choice:** 3-20 outcome options
3. **Over/Under:** Numeric predictions (viewership, duration, scores)
4. **Exacta:** Predict exact outcome (Top 2, Top 3)
5. **Prop Bets:** Fun side bets on random moments

### Bet Limits
- **Minimum Bet:** ₦50 per prediction
- **Maximum Bet:** ₦500,000 per prediction
- **Maximum Payout:** ₦10,000,000 per event

### Odds Ranges
- **Heavy Favorites:** 1.10 - 1.50 odds
- **Moderate Favorites:** 1.50 - 2.50 odds
- **Underdogs:** 2.50 - 10.00 odds
- **Long Shots:** 10.00 - 100.00+ odds

### Settlement
- **Live Events:** Settled within 5 minutes of conclusion
